Our Demographics

Age Range

Our members range from 20 to 70 years old, with the majority falling between 30-55. We believe that meaningful relationships can form at any stage of life.

Faith Background

While our members come from various faith backgrounds, all share a personal commitment to their spiritual principles and values. We welcome individuals from diverse faith traditions who prioritize their spiritual life and seek a partner who shares their commitment.

Professional Background

Our members represent diverse professional fields, including business, education, healthcare, ministry, arts, and more. What unites them is a sense of purpose in their work and a desire to use their gifts to make a positive impact.

Relationship Goals

All our members are seeking serious, marriage-oriented relationships. Whether never married, widowed, or divorced under biblically permissible circumstances, they share a commitment to building a God-honoring marriage.
