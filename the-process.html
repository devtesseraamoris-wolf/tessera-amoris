The Process

Our thoughtful approach to creating meaningful connections

The Legacy Framework

At Tessera Amoris, we've developed a proprietary approach to matchmaking that goes beyond superficial compatibility. Our Legacy Framework is built on three pillars that ensure meaningful, lasting connections between individuals who share not just attraction, but purpose.

Circle of Trust

Our rigorous verification process ensures that every member is who they claim to be. We conduct thorough background checks, personal interviews, and reference verifications to create a community built on authenticity and trust.

  • Identity verification through government-issued ID
  • Background checks for safety and security
  • Personal interviews with our matchmaking team
  • Personal references (e.g., from a spiritual leader, mentor, long-time friend, or family member)
  • Social media and online presence review

Cultural Harmony

We specialize in connecting individuals across cultural boundaries, particularly between Paraguay and Europe. Our approach celebrates the richness of diverse backgrounds while ensuring alignment on core Christian values and life goals.

  • Cultural orientation and education
  • Language support when needed
  • Identification of complementary cultural values
  • Family integration guidance
  • Cross-cultural communication coaching

Partnership with Purpose

Beyond romantic connection, we focus on matching individuals whose life purposes complement each other. We believe that the strongest relationships are those where partners support each other's calling and build a meaningful legacy together.

  • Vision and purpose assessment
  • Complementary gifting identification
  • Life mission alignment
  • Family vision planning
  • Legacy building guidance

Your Journey with Tessera Amoris

1

Application

Your journey begins with a comprehensive application that explores your background, faith, values, and relationship goals. This helps us understand who you are and what you're seeking in a partner.

2

Verification

Our team conducts thorough verification of your identity, background, and references. This step ensures the integrity of our community and builds trust among members.

3

Personal Interview

A one-on-one interview with a Tessera Amoris matchmaker helps us understand your personality, communication style, and deeper desires for a relationship that may not be captured in written form.

4

Profile Creation

We create a comprehensive profile that highlights your unique qualities, values, and purpose. This profile is only shared with potential matches who meet your criteria.

5

Thoughtful Matching

Unlike algorithm-based services, our experienced matchmakers personally select potential matches based on compatibility across multiple dimensions, with special attention to spiritual alignment and complementary purpose.

6

Guided Introduction

When both parties express interest, we facilitate a thoughtful introduction. This may begin with exchanging letters or a video call, depending on your preferences and circumstances.

7

Relationship Development

As your relationship develops, we provide resources, guidance, and support to help you navigate cultural differences, deepen your connection, and address any challenges that arise.

8

Continuous Mentoring

Even after a successful match, we continue to offer mentoring and resources to support your relationship's growth and help you build a strong foundation for marriage.

Our Guarantees

100% Verified Members

Every member of our community has undergone our rigorous verification process. You can be confident that the people you meet are who they claim to be.

Privacy Protection

Your personal information is kept strictly confidential. We never share your details with third parties, and your profile is only visible to potential matches approved by our team.

Faith-Centered Approach

Our entire process is guided by biblical principles and a commitment to honoring God's design for relationships. We pray over our matching process and seek divine guidance in our work.

Personalized Support

You're never alone in this journey. Our team of experienced matchmakers and relationship coaches is available to provide guidance, answer questions, and offer support throughout the process.

Ready to Begin?

Your journey toward a purposeful, God-honoring relationship starts with a simple step—completing your application. Our team is ready to guide you through each stage of the process with care and attention to your unique needs.
