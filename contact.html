Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the application process take?

The initial application review typically takes 3-5 business days. The complete verification process, including background checks and references, usually takes 2-3 weeks. We prioritize thoroughness over speed to ensure the integrity of our community.

What are your membership fees?

Our membership structure varies based on the level of service and support you require. We offer several tiers of membership, ranging from basic matching services to comprehensive support including cultural orientation, language assistance, and relationship coaching. Please contact us directly for current pricing information.

How do you verify members' identities?

We use a multi-layered verification process that includes government-issued ID verification, background checks, personal interviews, and reference checks. We also verify employment or business ownership and conduct social media reviews to ensure consistency in how members present themselves.

Do you work with clients outside of Paraguay and Europe?

While our primary focus is connecting individuals from Paraguay and Europe, we do consider applications from other regions on a case-by-case basis. The key requirement is alignment with our core values and a genuine desire for a purpose-driven, faith-centered relationship.

What denominations do you work with?

We work with members from various Christian denominations, including Catholic, Protestant, Orthodox, and non-denominational believers. What's most important is a personal commitment to Christ and alignment with core biblical values regarding marriage and relationships.

Ready to Begin Your Journey?

If you're ready to take the first step toward finding a purpose-driven relationship, we invite you to complete our application. Our team will guide you through each stage of the process with care and attention to your unique needs.
